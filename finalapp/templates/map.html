Dataset Map: Filter your selection below

This is a map of almost all 5,000 rows of data, with each location color-coded by the closing price per square foot. Exclusions were a few outlier homes that threw off the coloring for the entire map. The tooltip includes the address, closing data, closing price, total square footage, price per square foot, and days on market. It shows that the most expensive homes per square foot are squarely in the middle of Dallas, around Highland Park, as well as out by Southlake.
