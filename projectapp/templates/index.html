Dallas-Fort Worth Housing Market 2022



Texas is experiencing a steady rise in the price of homes and rent in recent months. We have used NTREIS (North Texas Real Estate Information) to predict the closing price and days on the market of a home in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. Our prediction model targets those who are interested in selling their home in the current market.

Questions to answer as you explore the website:
  • What will my house sell for with X and Y features?
  • How long should it take to sell my house in the current market?
  • What's the highest price per square foot that was sold in the last 2 months?
  • What house sold for the lowest closing price of those that have an HOA?
  • What county has the most young people (age 35 and under)?
  • What county has the highest per capita income?

Our data is limited to 5000 rows of March, April, and May 2022 sold homes in specific counties.
